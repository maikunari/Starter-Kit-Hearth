Our Story

A Quarter Century of Warmth

In 1999, Tom and Sarah Maplewood had a simple vision: to bring the warmth and comfort of beautiful fireplaces to every home in Cedar Falls. What started as a two-person operation in their garage has grown into the Cedar Valley's most trusted fireplace specialists, but we've never forgotten our roots as a family business that puts relationships first.

Today, our team of 7 skilled professionals brings decades of combined experience to every project. From our certified chimney sweeps to our master installers, each team member shares our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and honest service. We're not just colleagues – we're a family, and we treat every customer like part of that family.

"When we started this business, we promised ourselves we'd never compromise on quality or integrity. 25 years later, that promise still guides everything we do."

Tom & Sarah Maplewood Owners & Founders

Community Commitment

Proud Members of Cedar Falls

Being part of the Cedar Falls community means more than just running a business here – it means giving back. Over the years, we've been proud sponsors of Cedar Falls High School athletics, supporters of the local food bank, and active participants in community events like the Sturgis Falls Celebration.

Our team volunteers regularly with Habitat for Humanity, donating our fireplace expertise to help build homes for families in need. We believe that when our community thrives, we all thrive. That's why we source materials locally when possible, hire from within our community, and support other Cedar Falls small businesses.
